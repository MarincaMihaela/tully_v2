TULLY is a new paradigm in ADHD treatment, integrating the well-established principles of biofeedback in a manner that children can easily engage with.

The discrete sensors in the bracelet monitors the level of agitation a child is experiencing. Real time data processing allows, for the first time, a perfectly timed behavioral modification intervention, with much better results than dealing with the post factum effects of an unchecked hyperactivity event.
